How to Use the History API

By Dev · Demo article

This page ensures that pressing your browser’s Back button returns you to the homepage even if you arrived here directly.

On load, we:

  1. Insert a guard history entry so there’s something to “pop” back to.
  2. Intercept popstate and route to index.html.

Click your browser’s Back button (or the Back button above) to see it in action.
